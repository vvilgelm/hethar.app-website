privacy

presence requires awareness. here's what we log, what we don't, and why.

what we collect

  • Analytics: Page views, button clicks, navigation events via Plausible (no cookies, GDPR-compliant)
  • Contact form: Email or phone you provide, timestamp, source (breach site)
  • IP address: Only for rate limiting (3 submissions/hour). Not stored permanently.
  • Geolocation: Only if you explicitly allow. Used once to show proximity message. Not stored.

what we DON'T collect

  • ❌ No tracking cookies
  • ❌ No third-party advertising trackers
  • ❌ No keystrokes or form inputs (except submitted contact)
  • ❌ No microphone or camera access
  • ❌ No session recordings (cursor trails are local-only)
  • ❌ No sale of data to third parties

local-only features

These run entirely in your browser. Nothing leaves your device:

  • Shadow cursor (predictive movement)
  • Memory residue (cursor trail canvas)
  • Temporal rewind (session recording)
  • Behavior whispers (idle detection)
  • localStorage (skip intro, last visit timestamp)

geolocation (optional)

If you click "allow" on the proximity card:

  • We request your lat/long once via browser Geolocation API
  • We calculate distance to SF/NYC/etc. client-side
  • We show a single proximity message if within 120km
  • Your coordinates are NOT sent to our servers
  • Your location is NOT stored or logged

cookies & localStorage

We use localStorage (not cookies) to remember:

  • hethar_skip_intro - whether you've skipped the intro
  • hethar_last_visit - timestamp of your last visit
  • hethar_geo_asked - whether we've shown the geo prompt
  • hethar_paused - whether presence features are paused

sessionStorage (cleared when you close tab):

  • hethar_session_whisper - whether we've shown a whisper this session

your rights

  • Pause: Click "presence: on" (top-left) to disable all tracking
  • Clear: Clear browser cache/localStorage to reset
  • Opt-out: Disable JavaScript or use privacy mode
  • Contact removal: Email us to remove your contact from our list

data retention

  • Analytics: 2 years (Plausible default)
  • Contact submissions: Indefinitely (unless you request removal)
  • IP addresses: Not permanently stored (used only for rate limiting)

changes to this policy

We'll update this page if our privacy practices change. Last updated: October 4, 2025.
