Questions, answered.

Is this just Agent Mode with nicer UI? ＋

No. Agent Mode waits for commands. Hethar maintains a continuous model of your life, plans with constraints, and acts autonomously with guardrails, proofs, and rollbacks.

How does it know what I actually want? ＋

It learns longitudinally. It distinguishes short‑term impulses from durable values using outcomes and counterfactuals — and it shows its reasoning before acting.

What if it makes a mistake? ＋

Every action ships with a diff and a compensating action. You can undo in one tap; Hethar replays the ledger and updates its policy.

Do you store my private data in the cloud? ＋

Crown‑jewel data lives locally. Cloud workloads use summaries/embeddings when required. You can nuke everything anytime and receive a deletion proof.

Can it move money or sign on my behalf? ＋

Only within explicit caps and whitelists. Actions are per‑domain signed with your keys and fully auditable by you or a third party.

What can I try today? ＋

The Shadow Day demo: connect calendar/email; Hethar generates a simulated day plan, diffs, and outcomes vs. your baseline — no autonomous actions required.
